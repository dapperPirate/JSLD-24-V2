Jack Shorter

Lighting Designer & Programmer

Based in Surrey and Hampshire, Jack Shorter is a theatre practitioner whose primary work involves the design, programming and operation of simple to complex lighting rigs.

Jack has successfully designed and programmed lighting on a variety of Plays and Musicals, commonly with especially tight turnarounds for technical departments.

He can be contacted on this email address: jack@jackshorterlightingdesign.co.uk
